Life of Rosalind Franklin

  • 1920: Born in Notting Hill, London, Uk
  • 1941: Graduated from Newnham College
  • 1945: Earned her Ph.D.
  • 1947: Started her post-doc in Paris at the Laboratoire Central des Services Chimiques de l`Etat
  • 1951: Became a research associate at King's College London where she did her work on DNA
  • 1953: Moved to Birkbeck College
  • 1958: Died of ovarian cancer
